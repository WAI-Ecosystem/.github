# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard
We are committed to building a respectful, constructive, and professional environment for everyone contributing to WAI Ecosystem.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and professional
- Give constructive feedback
- Focus on solutions, not personal attacks
- Keep discussions relevant and helpful

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Sharing private or sensitive information without permission

## Enforcement
Organization maintainers may remove, edit, or moderate comments, issues, pull requests, or discussions that violate these standards.
